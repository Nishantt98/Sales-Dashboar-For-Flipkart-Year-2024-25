# Sales-Dashboar-For-Flipkart-Year-2024
Introduction: Sales Performance Report (2024-25)

1. Financial Health Overview
The company has maintained a healthy profit margin for the fiscal year 2024-25, driven by strong sales in the computing hardware sector.

Gross Revenue: The total sales reached 99M, demonstrating robust market demand.
Profitability: With a total cost of 76M, the company achieved a net profit of 23M.
Operational Volume: These figures were generated across 5K total transactions involving 13K individual units.

2. Product and Brand Performance
Market share is currently led by major hardware manufacturers, with a heavy skew toward core PC components.
Top Brands: Intel is the primary revenue driver, accounting for 18.89% (19M) of total sales. Asus (16.28%) and MSI (14.34%) follow as significant contributors.
Top Categories: Monitors are the highest-volume product with 1,843 units sold. High turnover is also seen in peripheral devices like Mice (1,496 units) and CPUs (1,450 units).

3. Regional and Personnel Insights
Geographic Reach: Sales are distributed across India, with a map-based visualization indicating high-density clusters in several states.
Sales Force Efficiency: The dashboard tracks performance across a dedicated team including Aadil Khan, Aarvi Gupta, Advika Joshi, Ajay Sharma, Roshan Kumar, and Vijay Singh.

4. Strategic Recommendations
Optimize High-Margin Brands: Since Intel and Asus represent over a third of total sales, consider establishing closer vendor partnerships to reduce the 76M total cost.
Inventory Alignment: Focus stock replenishment on Monitors and CPUs, which are the current volume leaders.
Regional Expansion: Use the "Sales by State" data to identify low-performing regions for targeted marketing campaigns.
